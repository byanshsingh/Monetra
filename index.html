Everything you need

Built for real personal finance

From budgeting to goal-setting, Monetra covers every aspect of your financial life in a single app.

💸

Transaction Tracking

Log income and expenses in seconds. Filter by type, search by keyword, and see your full history with clean visual cards.

🎯

Savings Goals

Create goals like "Buy a Laptop" or "Emergency Fund", track your progress with visual bars, and add money whenever you're ready.

📊

Smart Budgets

Set monthly budgets per category and watch live progress bars show how close you are to your limit — with early warnings.

🔁

Recurring Subscriptions

Mark any expense as recurring and Monetra automatically logs it each month. See your total monthly subscription cost at a glance.

📈

Analytics & Insights

Visual charts for income vs expenses, savings growth, spending by category, and monthly trends — all powered by Chart.js.

🔒

100% Private

All your data stays on your own device via localStorage. No login, no server, no ads. Export a JSON backup anytime.
